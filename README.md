# Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes customer shopping behavior to identify **revenue drivers**, **high-value customer segments**, and **growth opportunities**.  
The goal is to help businesses move from assumptions to **data-driven decision-making**.

The analysis focuses on understanding:
- Which product categories generate the most revenue  
- Which customer segments contribute the highest value  
- How subscriptions influence customer spending  

---

## Business Problem
Businesses often struggle to clearly understand:
- Where revenue is coming from  
- Who their most valuable customers are  
- Whether subscriptions and discounts actually improve performance  

Without these insights, marketing and pricing decisions remain inefficient and reactive.

---

## Dataset Summary
- **Total Records:** 3,900 customer transactions  
- **Data Type:** Transactional shopping data  
- **Includes:**  
  - Customer demographics  
  - Purchase behavior  
  - Subscription status  
  - Ratings and discount information  

The dataset allows customer behavior to be directly linked with business outcomes.

---

## Analysis Approach
The project follows an end-to-end, decision-focused approach:
- Data was prepared to ensure accuracy and consistency  
- Business questions were defined before analysis  
- Insights were translated into clear business impact  

The focus throughout the project is **insights and decisions**, not just numbers.

---

## Dashboard Overview
An interactive dashboard was created to give decision-makers a single view of customer behavior.

### Key Metrics
- Total number of customers  
- Average purchase value  
- Average customer rating  

### Key Visuals Explained
- **Revenue by Category:** Identifies the strongest revenue-generating product categories  
- **Sales by Age Group:** Highlights high-value customer segments  
- **Subscription Status:** Reveals opportunities to increase customer lifetime value  


---

## Key Insights
- Clothing and accessories are the top revenue-driving categories  
- Young adults are the highest-spending customer segment  
- Subscribers consistently show higher spending behavior  
- Discounts increase sales volume but require careful margin control  

---

## Business Impact
This analysis enables the business to:
- Focus marketing on high-value customer segments  
- Prioritize high-performing product categories  
- Use subscriptions as a revenue growth lever  
- Optimize discount strategies to protect profitability  

Overall, the project supports **revenue growth, customer retention, and smarter business planning**.
